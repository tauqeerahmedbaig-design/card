PUBG

PUBG is a popular battle royale game where players use strategy and skills to survive. The goal is to be the last player or team standing on the battlefield.

ROYAL PASS

The Royal Pass in PUBG gives players exclusive outfits and rewards. By completing missions, players can level up and unlock premium items.

UC

UC (Unknown Cash) is the premium currency used in PUBG. It allows players to buy Royal Passes, skins, and exclusive items.
